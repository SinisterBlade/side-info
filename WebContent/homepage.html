About Orange Juice!

Orange Juice! is a brand new way to browse the internet!

Orange Juice! uses the results of existing Search Engines and combines them with a well renowned clustering algorithm known as K-Means. Unlike traditional Search Engines, Orange Juice! shows links segregated into different clusters. All links within the same cluster can be described to be more similar to each other than links from different clusters. Hence, it goes without saying that users interested in a particular page may benefit from visiting the other links from the cluster.

Our Values

MISSION: Our mission is to provide a novel way to search for information on the Internet.

VISION: Our vision stems from the basic motivation to provide users a simpler way to obtain information from the Internet. In order to provide relevant information to the user, we crawl through web pages using an in-depth approach. We use Side Information obtained in the initial seed pages and try to find additional resources. Even though better ways to analyze text data exist such as Natural Language Processing, these are generally very computation heavy. In order to deliver effective results faster, we rely on the methods employed by existing Search Engines and combine it with a fairly fast K Means approach.
